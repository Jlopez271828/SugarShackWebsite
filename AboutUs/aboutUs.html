Sugar Shack is a family business that seeks to bring the you quaility and delicious home-made baked goods!

We are currently a mobile buisiness. We usually sell on the street and at events. We hope to establish a permanant location in the future!

In addition to our breads, we often sell many other snacks and treats. Our selection changes every week, so be sure to stop by to see what we have to offer!
